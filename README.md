This repository contains a college chatbot project. The chatbot is designed to assist students with various tasks related to their college life, such as providing information about courses, campus events, and administrative procedures.

Main Function Points
Provide information about college courses, schedules, and academic programs
Help students with administrative tasks like registration, financial aid, and campus resources
Offer guidance on extracurricular activities, student organizations, and campus events
Answer frequently asked questions and provide general support to college students
